## What is DACI?

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **D** - Driver | The person responsible for driving the decision process, gathering input, and ensuring a decision is made |
| **A** - Approver | The one person who makes the final call (there should be only one) |
| **C** - Contributors | People who provide input and expertise to inform the decision |
| **I** - Informed | People who need to know about the decision but aren't part of making it |

## Tips for Effective DACI

1. **One Approver only** - If multiple people need to approve, you have a process problem
2. **Driver ≠ Approver** - Separating these roles reduces bias
3. **Time-box decisions** - Set a due date to avoid analysis paralysis
4. **Document the "why"** - Future you will thank present you
5. **Inform early** - Don't surprise stakeholders with decisions
